Mindful Mail

MindfulMail is a simple service that will use OpenAI to remove harassing, traumatic and offensive language from emails and return a summary for you. It doesnt store any emails and is simple to use.

To use this first Sign in with Google below (just once) and after that you can simply send an email to help@mindfulmail.net or create a rule in your email client to forward mail you need to be summarised and MindfulMail will do the rest. You may need to check your spam filter for mail coming back from help@mindfulmail.net.

If you have any questions or concerns please contact @AndrewMohawk or mindfulmail@andrewmohawk.com :)
